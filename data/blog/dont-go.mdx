title: 'Don’t Go'
date: '2019-10-11'

summary: 'I’d love you to come and sit. And wonder more than ever, As you see this unearthly moonlit.'
---

I’d love you to come and sit. <br/>
And wonder more than ever, <br/>
As you see this unearthly moonlit. <br/>
And pass together, <br/>
Enchanting each other, <br/>
(Or silently rather?) <br/>
A few moments or so, <br/>
Before you’ll need to go. <br/>
