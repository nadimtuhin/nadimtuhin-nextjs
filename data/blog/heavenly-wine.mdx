If your love is my death, <br/>
I would refuse to live. <br/>
And that would be better, <br/>
Than those thousands of breaths, <br/>
I would breathe. <br/>

And if your eyes are, <br/>
Ocean of cries, <br/>
I would regret to smile. <br/>
I would prefer drinking that ocean, <br/>
To heavenly wine.
